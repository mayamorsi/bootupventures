BootUP World: The Global Startup Ecosystem

Welcome to BootUP world. Our building is a 25,000 square foot Global Startup Ecosystem located in the heart of Silicon Valley. In addition to monthly desk and office space for rent, amenities include high speed wifi, a complimentary printing allowance and access to seven conference rooms. Our facility also features rental space for meetings, presentations and events. We even have 12 beam devices located on-site making it easy for your out of town colleagues to join you at BootUP remotely!

Attached to our facility is our high end Cafe named the Cuckoo’s Nest after the “crazy” ideas turned into large corporations that are the backbone of Silicon Valley. At the Nest we hold regular events aimed at expanding entrepreneurial activity, including demo and pitch nights for startups as well as industry leading keynote speakers. We are located conveniently close to the Menlo Park Caltrain station as well as highway 101.
